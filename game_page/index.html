Star Wars: Battlefront

Description

Star Wars™ Battlefront™ is the ultimate Star Wars battle experience, brought to life by the creators of Battlefield.
Fight in epic Star Wars battles on iconic planets and rise through the ranks playing as the heroic Rebellion or the evil Galactic Empire.

Publishing Info

  • Published by: Electronic Arts, Inc
  • Developed by: EA Digital Illusions CE AB
  • Released: Nov 17 2015

Game Categories

  • Genre: Action
  • Perspective: 1st-person, behind view
  • Interface: Direct Control
  • Gameplay: Shooter
